Keep Away

Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.

Avoid Facial Contacts

Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth.

Use Mask

Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible. Coronavirus usually spreads through cough and covering your nose and mouth will save you from this epidemic.Use anti-pollution masks when out with friends or family.

Things You Shouldn't Do

Close Contact With Anyone

Do not get close to anyone, especially touching or laughing closely.

Spitting in Open

Spitting can increase the spread of the virus.

Using Public Transport

Travelling by cab, flight, bus, train and tramp can get you infected.

Use Over The Counter Medicines

If you are suffering from dry cough, back pain, nausea and shortness of breath it is recommended that you consult a doctor rather than opt for self-medication that can leave you battling with a deadly illness.
